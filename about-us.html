About Us

Welcome to Precious Meals and Bakes Private Limited, where every dish is crafted with passion, creativity, and a commitment to healthy, diverse eating. Founded in 2021 by Chef Precious, our catering company is based in Damofalls Park, Harare, specializing in wholesome, flavorful meals and baked goods for all occasions. At Precious Meals and Bakes, we believe that food should not only nourish but also inspire. Chef Precious has always loved cooking and baking for those who embrace variety and healthier choices, making this the driving force behind every recipe. From beautifully plated gourmet meals to indulgent yet balanced treats, we ensure that every bite is an experience. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering, a corporate event, or a grand celebration, we bring the perfect blend of taste, nutrition, and artistry to your table.
